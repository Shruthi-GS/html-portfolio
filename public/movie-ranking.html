The Best Movies/Dramas According to Shruthi

My top 5 movies/dramas of all time.

3 Idiots

This is one of my favourite Bollywood movie. It's about true friendship and life of Engineering students. A must-watch movie.

Dangal

A sports movie based on the real life women wrestlers Geeta and Babita Phogat. It's very inspiring and motivating.A highly recommended movie to watch.

Rush Hour

This is my favourite Jackie Chan movie. The comedy in it is top tier.

Squid Game

This is my favourite K-drama.It's very thrilling and a must-watch for all the thrill-seekers.

Train to Busan

This is my favourite Korean movie. It's extremely thrilling and keeps you at the edge of your seat throughout the movie.
